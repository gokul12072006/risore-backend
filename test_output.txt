## Current Steam Events
As of my knowledge cutoff, Steam hosts various events throughout the year. Here are some of the notable ones:

### Steam Next Fest
Steam Next Fest is an ongoing event where Steam automatically selects and invites eligible games that align with the theme of the sale. Partners have six weeks or more to register for the event and enter desired discounts.

### Steam-Wide Seasonal Sale Events
Steam runs four seasonal sale events per year, where any game released at least 30 days before the event start date can participate with a discount. Developers can enter and submit their discounts via the Discount Management page.

### Other Events
In addition to these events, Steam also features other promotions and sales, such as:

* **Trending Games**: A section showcasing games with significant increases in player count, like Warborne Above Ashes, which has seen a +889.8% increase in the last 24 hours.
* **New Releases**: A section highlighting newly released games, like The Invincible.

To stay up-to-date with the latest Steam events, I recommend checking the Steam website or following Steam's social media channels.
